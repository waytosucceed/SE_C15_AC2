Introduction to Percentages

A percentage is a mathematical concept that represents a number as a fraction of 100. The term “percentage” comes from the Latin “per centum,” meaning “by the hundred.” The symbol for percentage is “%.” Understanding percentages is fundamental in many areas, including finance, statistics, and general arithmetic.

Percentage :

A percentage is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100. The symbol used for percentage is “%”. For example, saying “25%” is equivalent to 25100 or 25 per 100.

Converting Percentages to Fractions

To convert a percentage to a fraction, simply write the percentage number over 100 and simplify if necessary.

Example 1:

Convert 75% to a fraction.

75100 = 75100
Simplify the fraction by dividing both the numerator and the denominator by their greatest common divisor, which is 25 in this case:
75100 = 75 ÷ 25100 ÷ 25 = 34

Converting Fractions to Percentages

To convert a fraction to a percentage, multiply the fraction by 100 and add the % sign.

Example 2:

Convert 34 to a percentage.
Multiply by 100 to get the percentage: (34 × 100)% = 3004% = 75%.

Converting Percentages to Decimals

To convert a percentage into a decimal, simply divide the percentage by 100. Then divide numerator by denominator to get the decimal number. This is equivalent to moving the decimal point two places to the left.

Steps to Convert from Percentage to Decimal:

  1. Take the percentage value.
  2. Divide by 100 or move the decimal point two places to the left.

Example:

Convert 75% to a decimal.

  • 75% means 75 per 100.
  • As a decimal: 75100 = 0.75

Converting Decimals to Percentages

To convert a decimal to a percentage, do the reverse of the conversion from percentage to decimal: multiply the decimal by 100. This effectively moves the decimal point two places to the right, turning it into a percentage.

Steps to Convert from Decimal to Percentage:

  1. Take the decimal number.
  2. Multiply by 100.
  3. Add the percentage sign (%) at the end.

Example:

Convert 0.32 to a percentage.

  • Multiply 0.32 by 100: (0.32 × 100)% = 32%
